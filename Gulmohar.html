Gulmohar

Scientific Name-Delonix regia
Genus- Delonix
Species- D. regia
Family- Leguminosae
Order- Fabales
Class- Spermatopsida
Division- Magnoliophyta
Kingdom- Plantae
Origin- Madagascar, the British brought it to India and grew it where they lived in colonies.
Description- The Gulmohar tree is a large, flowering, deciduous tree with a wide, umbrella-shaped crown. It can grow up to 30m tall, with a spread of 10-12m. The trunk is sturdy and can grow up to 2m in diameter, with a smooth, greyish-brown dark.

Cultivation- By seeds, semi-hardwood cutting.The Gulmohar tree needs 5 to 12 years to mature from seed before it may begin to bloom.The best time to plant a Gulmohar tree is in the spring or fall when temperatures are moderate and the tree can establish roots before hot weather sets in.

Medicinal Uses- It is used as Antimicrobial, Anti-diarrhea, Hepatoprotective, Antiinflammatory, Antioxidant, Antidiabetic, Antibacterial, Carminative, Antipyretic.

Variety- Flamboyant, Gold mohur, Rosea.
