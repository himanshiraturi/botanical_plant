White Champa Tree

Scientific Name-Magnolia alba
Genus-Magnolia
Family-Magnoliaceae
Order-Gentianales
Kingdom- Plantae
Species-M. alba
Origin-The White Champa tree, also known as Plumeria alba, is native to warm-temperate, subtropical, and tropical regions of Mexico, polynesia and Central America. It is a tropical shrub or small tree that is characterized by fragrant flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including white, yellow, pink, red and purple.
propagation -The White Champatree, also known as Plumeria or Champa, can be propagated by cuttings or seeds. Propagated by cuttings or seeds. Propagated by cuttings is the easiest and most preferred methods.
Description-It is also known as the joy perfume tree, is a medium-large, tropical evergreen tree that can grow to a height of 50ft or more. The tree is native to Indonesia, India, and other parts of Southern Asia. The tree is also known as Magnolia Champaca or Michelia Champaca. It has long, inverted, lance-shaped flowers that can be found in a range of colours from creamy white to orange.
Use-It is highly admired by the elite of Ayurveda. The root bark is considered as purgative, alterative and detergent. It is used to treat blennorrhagia and herpes.
Variety-Pulmeria alba, Plumeria rubra, Plumeria Pudica
