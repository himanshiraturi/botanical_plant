JackFruit

Scientific Name-Artocarpus heterophyllus
Genus -Artocarpus
Family -Moraceae
Order -Rosales
Class -Magnoliopsida
Division -Magnoliophyta
Species -A. heterophyllus
Origin - South western India, Bangladesh, Philippines and Sir Lanka.
Varieties -Black Gold, Cheena, Cochin, Dang, Rasimi, Golden Nugget, Golden Pillow etc.
Time Of Sowing -June-December

Description - The fruit is huge, seldom less than about 25cm in diameter. Even a relatively thin tree (circa 10cm) can have huge fruits hanging on it. The fruits can reach 36kg in weight and upto 90cm long and 50cm in diameter. The jackfruit is the largest tree borne fruit in the world.The sweet yellow sheaths around the seeds are about 3-5mm thick and have a taste similar to pineapple but milder and less juicy. The English name jackfruit derives from portuguese jaca, which is derived from Malayalam Chakka.
