Pygmy date palm

Scientific Name-Phoenix roebelenii
Genus-Phoenix
Family-Arecaceae
Order-Arecales
Class-Liliopsida
Kingdom- Plantae
Species-P. roebelenii
Origin-The pygmy date palm is native to southeastern Asia, including Southwestern China, northern Loas and northern Vietnam. It's also known as the dwarf date palm, miniature date palm, and robellini palm.
propagation -Pygmy date palm propagate through their cuttings. Around the base of the plant, release the soil around the sucker. seeds propagation, offshoot propagationand recentlydeveloped tissues culture techniques.
Description- It is a dwarf, evergreen palm tree that can grow to a maximum of 6 to 12ft tall. It has a slender trunk and a dense crown of fine-textured, lustrous green fronds. The fronds can grow to a maximum lenght of 3 to 4ft and from a dense evergreen canopy.
Use- The pygmy date palm is suitable for use indoors in cooler climates. It is usually used as a single specimen although it is also effective in groups of three or more.
Variety-Phoenix roebelenii, Coontie palm, Triangle palm, Dte palm etc.
