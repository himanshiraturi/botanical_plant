Mango

Scientific Name-Mangifera indica
Genus-Mangifera
Species- M. indica
Family-Anacardiaceae
Order-Sapindales
Class-Magnoliopsida
Division-Magnoliophyta
Kingdom- Plantae
Origin- Southeast Asia
Description- A mango is a sweet, tropical fruit that grows on trees in the cashew family.Mangoes are part of the drupe family and are roughly oval in shape. They have a thin, waxy skin that ranges in colourfrom yellow-green to red. The flesh is soft and yellow- orange in color, and there is a large pit in the middle. Mangoes have a sweet, tangy flavor. Mangoes are regarded as the king of fruits.

Cultivation- Veneer grafting, Inarching, Epicotyl grafting, Side grafting. Planting can be done in July-August in rainfed areas and during February-March in irrigated areas.
Medicinal Uses- Mango may help reduce weight in those fighting with obesity. Being a good source of carotene, it may help improve vision.
Variety- Mallika, Amrapali, Mangeer, Ratna, Arka Aruna, Arka Puneet, Arka Anmol.
