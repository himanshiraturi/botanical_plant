Marigold

Scientific Name- Calendula officinalis
Genus- Calendula
Family- Calenduleae
Order- Asrerales
Class- Magnoliopsida
Division- Magnoliophyta
Kingdom- Plantae
Hindi Name- Gainda
Origin- Valley of Flower, Ranthambore national park.
Other names- Caltha, Ganda, Gols bloom, Garden Marigold, Gold boom, Holligold, Marybud, Pot Marigold are the other names used for the Marigold.
Description- It flowers from July to September. The scented folwers are hermaphrodite (have both male and female organs). It is hardy to zone 6 and is frost tender. The lower leaves are broad and spatula shaped.

Cultivation- Marigold plant is propagated by the seeds. They are snown during the early spring. The seeds should be snown 2cm apart.

Medicinal Uses- Marigold is used for stomatch upset, ulcers, menstrual period problems, eye infection, inflammations etc.

Variety- African Marigold, Tagetes patula, Signet Marigold, French Marigold.
